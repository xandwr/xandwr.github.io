## The Process
First, I had to acquire the materials. Luckily, I managed to scrounge up some leftover parts from other projects:

Once I had my parts, I got to work.
<br>
<br> Before we move on, one thing to understand about these cartridges is that the outer ring (with the threads) 
is the ground connection, while the inner ring (with the hole) is the live connection. This means I had to deliver power
to the inside ring, and connect the threaded part to ground:
<figure class="full">
  <a href="/assets/images/Projects/Lightswitch Vape/threads.jpg">
    <img src="/assets/images/Projects/Lightswitch Vape/threads.jpg">
  </a>
</figure>

<figure class="full">
  <a href="/assets/images/Projects/Lightswitch Vape/jigged_elbow.jpg">
    <img src="/assets/images/Projects/Lightswitch Vape/jigged_elbow.jpg">
  </a>
  <figcaption>Ooh, bendy!</figcaption>
</figure>
