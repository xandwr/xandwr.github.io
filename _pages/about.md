## Who am I?
Great question. I ask myself that every day.
<br>
<br> What I can say for sure is that my name is Xander, and I'm a 21 y/o full-time student in Edmonton with a passion for creative chaos.

## What do I do?
Well, it depends on the day:
- **Coding** -- I build stuff. Sometimes it works, sometimes it *kinda* works, and sometimes I question my actions. But either way, I'm always learning something new!
- **Engineering** -- I like to build kooky stuff just to prove to myself that I can. But sometimes, just because you can, doesn't mean you should.
- **Music** -- My sweet release. Who wants to *feel* their emotions when they can just immortalize them in song and think, "*man, this is terrible...*"?
- **Photo/Video** -- Because sometimes it's worth having more than just memories.

## You must be one of those "recluses" then!
